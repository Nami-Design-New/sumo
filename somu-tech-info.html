ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING

Electrical engineering courses teach the use of electronics to create, convey, and manipulate information. You'll master the basics of circuits and signal processing, then move on to advanced subtopics such as microelectronics, telecommunications, and power and control engineering.

Our training courses cover most of the needed skills and competencies including:

  • Electrical Equipment and control
  • Electrical design
  • Electrical Installation
  • High Voltage switching
  • Low voltage switching
  • Power Generator
  • Generator excitation
  • Troubleshooting & Maintenance
